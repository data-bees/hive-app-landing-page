General FAQ

Who are we?

We are a market research company called Hatched Analytics. We are based in Dublin, Ireland and we have been around for 5 years.

What do we do?

We help our corporate clients make better investment decisions by providing them with insights about consumers' shopping behaviour.

Is this legi?

Yes!  We pay you so that we can better understand the companies you purchase from. Sound too good to be true? We perform market research with the goal of better understanding companies. We perform this research by collecting email receipt data.

Why are we collecting data?

Our goal is to better understand the companies you purchase from.  The data we collect from your receipts is added to our dataset, the Data Vault, which is summarized in company research reports that investors can then purchase. We never collect personal email data and we never share personal details of our panelists with our clients. We have never sold, do not sell, and will never sell data for advertising, profiling or reidentification purposes.

Why does my email service warn me that someone is using IMAP from another location?

We use IMAP to connect to your email inbox - no stress, everything is perfectly normal!

Application testing FAQ

What should I do after the testing is complete?

Please remove the app from your phone and delete the app password generated by the app from the Gmail settings after the testing is complete. We reserve the right to use the data collected during this testing in our research.
